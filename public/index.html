About Us

Our goal is to create an autonomous network of vehicles, able to provide logistic services to any client, on-demand. We are beginning our journey with a fleet of drones, and believe this service will supplement current methods of delivery for inventory, retail goods, and critical needs shipments.

Where we are at now

Our team is currently prototyping our system. We have been developing custom drones, coding up a drone control server, creating a mobile app for our users, and building wireless charging modules for our fleet.

Wireless Charging

KardashevOne has developed a wireless charging bay for our drones' convenience. These charging bays, called Voxels, use a patent pending design that allows a drone to locate and dock itself, receive a charge, then continue on its mission.

Custom Drones

We had to take conventional drone design back to the drawing board. Our drones each have an onboard computer used for communicating with the control server and locating Voxels. The fleet is also equipped with special landing gear that allows each drone to carry a payload, as well as interface with Voxels.

Intelligent Fleet Routing

Our engineers have developed a control station to delegate tasks to the drone fleet and route them to and from each task, minimizing delivery time.
